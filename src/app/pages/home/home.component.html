Keeping in mind the growing number of people challenged by eye health challenges like age-related muscular degeneration(AMD), cataracts, galucoma, myopia and diabetes related vision issues, regener-eyes team created a comprehensive solution for the problem with innovative solution.

Clear Vision 1.0

Clear Vision 1.0 is an electronic eye wear that is aesthetically pleasing, portable, cost effective and easy to use solution for people. Armed with patented technology, Clear Vision 1.0 improves visual clarity of people and empowering individuals to lead individual lives.

With Clear Vision 1.0, we strive to bring a smile to the lives of people with vision deficits.

Benefits with ClearVision 1.0

We leverage advanced technology to empower individuals to take control of their vision health, enabling them to prioritize what truly matters in life. Our user-friendly devices and services seamlessly integrate into everyday life, supporting improved results in managing vision health.
