Welcome to Regener-Eyes

We at regener-eyes strive to harness existing and cutting-edge technologies to develop solutions and improve the quality of life of people with vision challenges. Our efforts are to enable our customers to live more independent and productive lives.

The Challenge

  • There are millions of people in the US and rest of the world who have trouble seeing despite using glasses, contact lenses, various devices and other interventions. A majority of vision deficits are caused by AMD, glaucoma, cataracts and diabetic retinopathy etc. These numbers are expected to increase significantly as the population ages.

The Solution

  • Our first product is an innovative Near Eye Device (NED) that addresses the major vision deficits identified by leading ophthalmologists in a sleek, aesthetic and human-centered design. It is very easy to wear, use and has all the major functions required to enable the affected population to live more independently. Our Innovative product utilizes high end capabilities incorporating a unique integration of existing and proven technologies.
