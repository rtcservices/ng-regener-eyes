About Us

Founded by a diverse team of experts from ophthalmology, biotechnology, engineering, pharmaceuticals, and business sectors, seeks to enhance the lives of people with vision deficits.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve the quality of life of millions of vision-impaired people in the U.S. and across the globe.

Our Vision

To develop and market smart, innovative, and intuitive solutions that will restore independence to people with vision challenges.
